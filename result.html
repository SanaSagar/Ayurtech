Suggestions for Vata Dosha

{% elif dosha == "Pitta" %}

Suggestions for Pitta Dosha

Dietary Suggestions:
  • Favor cooling, hydrating foods like cucumbers, leafy greens, and melons.
  • Avoid spicy, salty, and fried foods as they increase heat in the body.
Lifestyle Suggestions:
  • Engage in cooling activities like swimming and avoid excessive heat.
  • Practice calming activities like meditation and stay hydrated.
{% elif dosha == "Kapha" %}

Suggestions for Kapha Dosha

Dietary Suggestions:
  • Favor light, warm, and spicy foods like leafy greens, lean proteins, and warming spices.
  • Avoid heavy, oily, and sugary foods like dairy, fried foods, and sweets.
Lifestyle Suggestions:
  • Stay active with dynamic exercise like jogging or cycling to avoid sluggishness.
  • Break up routine activities and avoid naps to maintain energy levels.
